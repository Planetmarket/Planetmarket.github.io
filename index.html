1. Contract farming marketplace

Contract farming provides two main advantages to farmers: they are guaranteed price and credit support. Commercial crops such as sugarcane, cotton, oilseeds, tea, coffee, and jute are cultivated in India on a large scale. each year farmers get manipulated by companies and factories due to Lack of standardized contract regulation and power imbalance.

Our contract platform enables farmers and buyers to create, negotiate, and sign agreements digitally. Features include automated reminders, document tracking, and secure storage. Reduce paperwork and improve transparency in agricultural transactions.

2. Direct consumer market

India is facing a huge scarcity of land, which results in small-scale production by farmers. In spite of land scarcity, marketing is also a huge problem. Intermediaries make up to 40 to 80% of the price paid by consumers. there is rquirement of central brand and an agricultural marketplace to sell value-added products to consumers in both farm and non-farm categories.

Our sales platform provides farmers with direct access to regional and international markets. List your products, manage inventory, process orders, and receive payments securely. Real-time market data helps you make informed pricing decisions.

3. Asset light transportation of goods

Agricultural goods are of different forms perishable, bulk, and high-volume it requires flexible transportation and handling. open transportation of goods with indivisual service providers and timely use of available vehicles with good co-ordination is necessary.

Coordinate transportation of perishable goods with our specialized logistics platform. Find verified carriers, track shipments in real-time, and ensure proper handling of temperature-sensitive products. Reduce spoilage and delivery delays with optimized routing.

4. Shared Infrastructure services & renting

Infrastructure such as roads, highways, internet, smartphones, and payment systems are significantly available in India. Other essential facilities such as processing, sorting, packaging, warehousing, and cold trucking chains need to be flexible, well-coordinated, and in small unit forms to cover large rural areas.

Our infrastructure platform connects farmers with storage facilities, processing units, and equipment rentals. Find available silos, cold storage, or processing facilities in your area. Share resources with other farmers to reduce costs and improve efficiency.
